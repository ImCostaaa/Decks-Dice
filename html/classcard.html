Detailed Description

Card class represents a playing card with a suit and value.

Card class provides functions to convert number values of cards to their string representation.
Face values are: 1 being Ace, 11 being Jack, 12 being Queen, 13 being King
Suit values are: 1 being Spade, 2 being Club, 3 being Heart, 4 being Diamond
